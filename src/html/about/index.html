The origin of signo

Born out of a lack of information or clarity in the field of screen accessibility, signo has been developed in a month.

signo encloses information which was gathered among many publications, such as W3C, WebAIM, Medium, Orange, Microsoft, Material.io, etc. This knowledge was rewritten in order to be easily understandable, and served in a way which aims to be widely spread.

Who's behind signo?

The signo initiative was brought to life by Mathieu Delvaux, a DWM student, as part of his final project.

Mathieu is a multidisciplinary designer from Belgium and currently studying in his final year at Albert Jacquard College.
He is also the Co-Founder & Creative UX Manager of nextmoov, the Co-Founder & Product Design Manager of NextRide and a former design intern at EPIC Agency.
