What is signo?

Signo is an initiative developed as a toolbox to help anyone design and develop accessible experiences for screens. Inclusiveness is currently not part of most of design processes, and that is what signo is aiming to change.

Accessiblity is what allows people with impairments to enjoy a meaningful screen experience, from their computer to their phone. Although guidelines have been defined, few people know about them. Crafting for everyone involves a particular understanding, which then comes as a habit.
